Welcome! Here are a few reviews relating to knickknacks.

Inkwood Books

Look for all kinds of books at this New Jersey small business located in Haddonfield. Their store holds all the best books, with knowledgeable staff to inform you of any book suggestions. They also host a unique event, the virtual book fair!

Make sure to check Inkwood Books out by going on their website.

Fern & Fossil

Need a gift but don't know what to get? Check out Fern & Fossil! They provide plants and terrariums that are fun and an interactive way to gift a little greenery. Fern & Fossil offers DIY kits alongside soy-based succulent candles and even custom terraria.

Make sure to shop this unique small business and check out their website Fern & Fossil!

A Place on Earth

Want amazingly scented shampoos, scrubs, sprays, soy candles, and more? Well, you'll find just that at this small business located in Cape May! These are perfect as gifts and are made with love and beneficial ingredients. The scents and fragrances also are inspired by Cape May itself.

Check this store out and learn more about this shop and other things to do in Cape May!
